I am **Jules Perret**, a PhD researcher and research engineer in gravitational astrophysics at the Astroparticle and Cosmology Laboratory (APC, CNRS). I work on data analysis for the LISA space mission, with a focus on Bayesian inference and advanced parameter estimation methods for compact binary systems, including binary neutron stars and binary black holes.

My research combines statistical inference, deep learning, and Hamiltonian-based sampling methods to accelerate and improve gravitational-wave parameter estimation. I have a strong interest in numerical simulations and high-performance computing, including large-scale parallel computing and GPU development for astrophysical applications.
